Our Vision



At UMANANDAN PLAY SCHOOL, INDORE, we recognize the imperative of imparting an educational experience that is state of the art in every respect and which moulds children for becoming a responsible citizen in future. We are a school with an Indian mind, an Indian heart and an Indian soul; a school that rejoices the ethos of excellence and is an epitome of values. We believe that the academic curriculum should be capable enough to make children ready to face the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and strength of character.
